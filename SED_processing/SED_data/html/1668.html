*g\xca\xb7afl-/*gVfn- - stuffed skin given in place of her young one to a bereaved mother-animal\n (EDG 266, DRS 170, Kogan 2015: 554)\n

\n
\n
\n\n
\n
\n MSA + Southern EthS. The ultimate origin of this peculiar areal isogloss is obscure.\r\nAny connection with Arb. \xc7\xafafn- \xe2\x80\x98scabbard, sheath\xe2\x80\x99? (Kogan 2015: 554)\n
