\n

*ks\xcb\x80 - to find\n (Kogan 2015:560\xe2\x80\x93561)\n

\n
\n
\n\n
\n
\n The origin of Proto-MSA *ks\xcb\x80 \xe2\x80\x98to find\xe2\x80\x99 is uncertain, although an ultimate connection with Arb. ks\xcb\x80 \xe2\x80\x98to pursue, to follow\xe2\x80\x99 (Lane 2608) is not unlikely. In such a case, Tgr. k\xc3\xa4s\xcb\x80a \xe2\x80\x98to drive to pasture\xe2\x80\x99 (WTS 407) must be related as well (unless borrowed from Arabic). M. H\xc3\xb6fner (1987:43) identified the MSA root with Qat. ks3\xcb\x80 in Q 243:4\xe2\x80\x925 (w-kl s1hmm w-\xe1\xb8\xb3nym b-ykn w-yks3\xcb\x80 ws1\xe1\xb9\xad \xe1\xb8\x8ftn \xcb\x80bytn \xe2\x80\x98All servants and property which are present and found in these houses\xe2\x80\x99), which is not improbable. (Kogan 2015:560-561)\n
