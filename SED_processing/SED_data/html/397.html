*piry- - fruit\n (Fronzaroli 1968: 276, 290, 300; Kogan 2011: 201; Kogan 2012: 233)\n

\n
\n
\n\n
\n
\n see also *pry to bear fruit \r\nThis root has to be separated from the eventually related terms with the\r\nsame biconsonantal sequence *pr, particularly from Akk. per\xcb\x80u \xe2\x80\x98shoot, offshoot\xe2\x80\x99, which cannot go back to *piry- for phonological\r\nreasons (Kogan 2012a:233)\n
