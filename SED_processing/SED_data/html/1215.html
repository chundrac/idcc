*\xcb\x81Vng(-at)- - neck\n (SED I 18)\n

\n
\n
\n\n
\n
\n Compared to Afras. *\xcb\x81Vng- \xe2\x80\x98neck, throat\xe2\x80\x99 in SED I. May be also attested in Ebla: in-gu /\xcb\x81ingu(m)/ or /\xcb\x81in\xe1\xb8\xb3u(m)/ \xe2\x80\x98neck\xe2\x80\x99, cf. SED I p. 18.\n
