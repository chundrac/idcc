*\xcb\x80iy\xc4\x81/\xcb\x80iy\xc4\x81t/y\xc4\x81t - nota accusativi\n (Arakelova 2001:37)\n

\n
\n
\n\n
\n
\n It is likely that this preposition was initially used only with pronominal suffixes (which is the state of affairs in most languages), but later also obtained the function of introducing a nominal object in Canaanite and Aramaic languages (Jou\xcc\x88on\xe2\x80\x93Muraoka 2005:344). MSA forms are explicable only if one assumes the loss of initial \xcb\x80. The nature of initial k in Geez kiy\xc4\x81- which most scholars trace back to this preposition remains uncertain (Arakelova 2001:38).\n
