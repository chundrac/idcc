*p\xc4\xabl-, *p\xc4\xabr- - elephant\n (SED II No. 173; Kogan 2011: 209)\n

\n
\n
\n\n
\n
\n Reflexes of *p\xc4\xabl- or *p\xc4\xabr- in individual languages are usually considered interborrowings going back to a non-Semitic source, but this analysis is hard to apply to Gez. falfal \xe2\x80\x98water buffalo; elephant\xe2\x80\x99 with its markedly different morphological shape.\n
