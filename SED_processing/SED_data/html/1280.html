*\xe1\xb8\xb3n\xcb\x80 - to be jealous\n (Kogan 2015: 88)\n

\n
\n
\n\n
\n
\n Possible attestations of this root in Arabic are problematic. Many scholars accepted its\r\nconnection with Arb. qn\xcb\x80 \xe2\x80\x98to be intensely red\xe2\x80\x99, very tentatively mentioned in Brockelmann 1932:115, but this is semantically rather far-fetched. Perhaps more promising as a cognate\r\nwould be Arb. qn\xcb\x80 \xe2\x80\x98to incite, to induce to kill someone\xe2\x80\x99 = \xe2\x80\x9cto kill out of envy\xe2\x80\x9d?\n
