*\xe1\xb8\xb3Vbl - before, in front of, opposite to\n (Arakelova 2001:47)\n

\n
\n
\n\n
\n
\n This preposition is considered to be derived from *\xe1\xb8\xb3bl \xe2\x80\x98to be in front of; to accept, to receive\xe2\x80\x99.\n
