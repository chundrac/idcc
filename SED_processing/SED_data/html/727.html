\n \n Akkadian\n \n
\n
\n \n \xe1\xb8\xabu\xe1\xb9\xa3annu - sash\n (CAD \xe1\xb8\xab 259, AHw 361)\n \n
\n
\n
\n\n
\n Little doubt this is a semantic shift from an unattested anatomical term (for the semantic development, cf. *\xe1\xb8\xa5i/a\xe1\xb8\xb3(\xca\xb7)\xc2\xad \xe2\x80\x98hip, loin, lap\xe2\x80\x99, No. 113); another meaning shift from \xe2\x80\x98bosom, lap\xe2\x80\x99 is likely represented by \xe1\xb8\xaba\xe1\xb9\xa3\xc4\x81nu \xe2\x80\x98to shelter, receive in a friendly way\xe2\x80\x99 SB on [CAD \xe1\xb8\xab 129], \xe2\x80\x98in den Arm nehmen\xe2\x80\x99 [AHw 331] (note \xe1\xb8\xab corresponding to Sem. *\xe1\xb8\xa5).\n
