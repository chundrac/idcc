*kVm(V)d- - forearm, cubit\n (SED I No. 144)\n

\n
\n
\n\n
\n
\n A phonetic diversity throughout Sem. can be explained by (1) lower compatibility of a voiced and voiceless stop in Hbr. and Arm., and (2) change of *\xc2\xadm\xc2\xad > \xc2\xadn\xc2\xad in contact with \xc2\xad-d (for a similar phonetic evolution cf. *p/ban/md(\xc2\xadat)\xc2\xad \xe2\x80\x98back, podex\xe2\x80\x99,\r\nNo. 216).\n
