\n \n Hebrew\n \n
\n
\n \n \xc5\xa1\xcb\x81l - (qal, hitp.) to cough\n (BY 7354)\n \n
\n
\n
\n\n
\n Attested only in Late Rabbinic Hebrew and considered by Ben Yehuda loans from Syriac [BY 7348]. This can hardly be proved; it is of interest, however, that no verb with a clear meaning \xe2\x80\x98to cough\xe2\x80\x99 is known in the whole corpus of Biblical and early post-Biblical texts.\n
