*\xc5\xa1itim- - heaven\n (Kogan 2015: 571)\n

\n
\n
\n\n
\n
\n The origin of Proto-MSA *\xc5\xa1itim- \xe2\x80\x98heaven\xe2\x80\x99 is enigmatic: an eventual connection\r\nwith PS *\xc5\xa1am\xc4\x81y- is difficult to avoid (Bittner 1909:42), but the underlying\r\nphonological and/or structural development has never been convincingly\r\nestablished (none of the hypotheses listed by Leslau in LS 78 is persuasive).\n
