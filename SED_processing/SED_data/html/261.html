\n\n
\n As for yw\xe1\xb8\xb3\xcb\x80 \xe2\x80\x98he will bring out\xe2\x80\x99 in KAI 215:21, this reading and interpretation are indeed widely accepted in recent studies on Samalian (Dion 1974:42, Tropper 1993a:130, KAI5), but in view of\r\nepigraphic and contextual difficulties connected with this passage no certainty about the functional status of the Samalian reflex of *w\xc5\x9d\xcb\x80 \xcc\xa3 is possible (Kogan 2015:411).\n
