*matn- (1) - sinew, tendon; nerve\n (Held 1965: 405; SED I No. 191; Kogan 2011: 215)\n

\n
\n
\n\n
\n
\n

Usually unseparated from the forms meaning \xe2\x80\x98hip, loin\xe2\x80\x99, etc. (see *matn- (2)\xc2\xa0- hip, loins, small of the back) which are semantically difficult to compare. Note, however, that a semantic contamination with metathetic *mV(\xcb\x80)n(-at)-\xc2\xa0- tendon, sinew; muscle\xc2\xa0(No. 183) is possible.\xc2\xa0
