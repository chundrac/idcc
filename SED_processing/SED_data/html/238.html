*\xe1\xb8\xa5\xc4\x81miy-(a)t- - wall\n (Blau 1957: 98, Marrassini 1971: 54\xe2\x80\x9256, Ginsberg 1973:134, Kogan 2015: 294)\n

\n
\n
\n\n
\n
\n is probably derived from PS *\xe1\xb8\xa5my \xe2\x80\x98to watch, to protect\xe2\x80\x99; to be reconstructed as an active participle *\xe1\xb8\xa5\xc4\x81miy-(a)t- on the joint evidence of Hebrew, El-Amarna and Ugaritic data\r\n if Arb \xe1\xb8\xa5\xc4\x81miyat- \xe2\x80\x98mass of stones with which a well is cased\xe2\x80\x99 and \xe1\xb8\xa5\xc4\x81miyeh - \xe2\x80\x98courtyard\xe2\x80\x99 are connected, it would push *\xe1\xb8\xa5\xc4\x81miy-(a)t- \xe2\x80\x98wall\xe2\x80\x99 back to PCS\n
