*kVma/kVm\xc4\x81 - like, in the manner of\n (Arakelova 2001:18)\n

\n
\n
\n\n
\n
\n This preposition is usually considered to be a variant of *kV (with the enclitic -ma). However, the fact that in most languages it is used alongside with *kV and that in several languages only forms of this type are attested allows to speak of the independent status of this preposition (Tropper 2000:781, Arakelova 2001:19\xe2\x80\x9320).\n
