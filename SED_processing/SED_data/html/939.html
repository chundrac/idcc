\n \n di\xcb\x80u - a grave disease characterized by a headache\n (CAD D 165, AHw. 174)\n \n

\n\n
\n Cf. dam\xc3\xbb \xe2\x80\x98to suffer from convulsions\xe2\x80\x99 SB (CAD D 80, AHw. 166), dim\xc4\xabtu \xe2\x80\x98a disease (paralysis, dizziness (?), poisoning of the flesh\xe2\x80\x99 CAD D 143. Though *VwV > VmV is normal for this period, these forms may also go back to the root *dmw/y (see *dwm \xe2\x80\x98to be giddy; have pain in the head\xe2\x80\x99, SED I No. 11v).\n
