*w\xe1\xb8\xb3d/*\xe1\xb8\xb3wd - to burn\n (Kogan 2015:398)\n

\n
\n
\n\n
\n
\n PS *w\xe1\xb8\xb3d/*\xe1\xb8\xb3wd \xe2\x80\x98to burn\xe2\x80\x99 is relatively well attested (DRS 601), but as opposed to Aramaic neither Hbr. y\xe1\xb8\xb3d, nor Akk. \xe1\xb8\xb3\xc3\xa2du, nor Arb. wqd can be said to function as the basic verbs with the meaning \xe2\x80\x9cto burn\xe2\x80\x9d (intransitive) in the respective languages.\n
