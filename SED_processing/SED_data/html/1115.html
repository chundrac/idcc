*\xcb\x80il\xc4\x81h- - god\n (Kogan 2015: 179)\n

\n
\n
\n\n
\n
\n

The origin of the -\xc4\x81h extension cannot be established with certainty, but as plausibly argued in Huehnergard 2005 (following Brockelmann 1908:334; cf. Pardee 1999:286, 2000:36), it may represent a back-formation from the plural *\xcb\x80il-\xc4\x81h-\xc5\xabma
