\n \n Akkadian\n \n
\n
\n \n li\xe1\xb8\xb3 p\xc3\xae - palate\n (CAD I 205, AHw. 555)\n \n
\n
\n
\n\n
\n Var. ali\xe1\xb8\xb3, ela\xe1\xb8\xb3 p\xc3\xae. Forms may be alternatively compared to Arb. la\xe1\xb8\xb3la\xe1\xb8\xb3\xc2\xad \xe2\x80\x98tongue\xe2\x80\x99 [BK 2\r\n1016]\n
\n
\n\n

\n \n Ugaritic\n \n
\n
\n \n \xe1\xb8\xa5l\xe1\xb8\xb3-m - throat(?)\n (DLU 176)\n \n
\n
\n
\n\n
\n In the following passage: t\xce\xb3ll bdm \xe1\xb8\x8fmr \xe1\xb8\xa5l\xe1\xb8\xb3m \xe2\x80\x98she plunged the throat into the blood of the warriors\xe2\x80\x99 (KTU 1.3 II 28). Here [DLU 176] also a discussion with various possibilities of translation quoted, \xe2\x80\x98neck, throat\xe2\x80\x99 among them\n
