*\xe1\xb8\xa5agal- - partridge\n (SED II No. 97)\n

\n
\n
\n\n
\n
\n The Common Semitic status of this root is uncertain since inter-Semitic borrowing are possible. Whereas an Arabism in Syriac is not very likely (the Syr. term is attested already in the Peshitta), an Arb. borrowing in Tgr. is quite probable.\n
