*\xe1\xb9\xa3\xcc\x82VpardV\xcb\x81- - frog\n (SED II No. 222; Kogan 2011: 210)\n

\n
\n
\n\n
\n
\n It is only the Hbr. form that reflects the original quinqueconsonantal base, reduced to *\xe1\xb9\xa3\xcc\x82VrdV\xcb\x81- in Aramaic and *\xe1\xb9\xa3\xcc\x82VpdV\xcb\x81- in Arabic and MSA. Alternatively, two unrelated quadriconsonantal terms can be postulated for PS with their subsequent contamination in Hebrew.\n
