\n \n Hebrew\n \n
\n
\n \n m\xc9\x99tall\xc9\x99\xcb\x81\xc5\x8dt - jawbones (pl.)\n (HALOT 654)\n \n
\n
\n
\n\n
\n

Metathetic from \xc2\xad-t\xc2\xad- infixed stem (cf. constr. *malt\xc9\x99\xcb\x81\xc5\x8dt in Ps 58.7). Cf. l\xc5\x8d\xc4\x83\xcb\x81, a hapax in Pr 23:2, translated as \xe2\x80\x98gullet\xe2\x80\x99 in [HALOT 532]. The context, however, allows for both interpretations, \xe2\x80\x98gullet\xe2\x80\x99 and \xe2\x80\x98jaw\xe2\x80\x99, the latter supported by the meaning \xe2\x80\x98jaw\xe2\x80\x99 attested in Hbr. pB. [Jastrow 700].
