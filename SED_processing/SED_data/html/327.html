\n \n Akkadian\n \n
\n
\n \n bu\xcb\x80\xc3\xbb - to look for, to search for\n (CAD B 360, AHw. 145)\n \n
\n
\n
\n\n
\n Mostly in Babylonian and, remarkably, never in Old Assyrian, where \xc5\xa1e\xcb\x80\xc4\x81\xcb\x80um is normally used instead (Kogan 2006c:195). CAD B 365 is right to point out that the meaning \xe2\x80\x9cto wish\xe2\x80\x9d for bu\xcb\x80\xcb\x80\xc3\xbb, widely attested in some peripheral varieties of Akkadian, is likely due to WS influence.\n
