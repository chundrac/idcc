*\xe1\xb8\xa5zl - to carry on the back\n (CDG 253; Kogan 2015: 460)\n

\n
\n
\n\n
\n
\n The etymology of Proto-EthS *\xe1\xb8\xa5zl \xe2\x80\x98to carry\xe2\x80\x99 is uncertain. Shall one tentatively compare Arb. \xe1\xb8\xa5a\xe1\xb8\x8fl- = \xe1\xb8\xa5u\xc7\xafzatu l- \xcb\x80iz\xc4\x81ri wa-l-qam\xc4\xab\xe1\xb9\xa3i wa-\xe1\xb9\xadarafuhu (LA 11 179), from an original meaning \xe2\x80\x9cplace of carrying\xe2\x80\x9d?\n
