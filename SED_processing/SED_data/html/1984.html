*tkb - to prick, to puncture\n (EDA II)\n

\n
\n
\n\n
\n
\n

Cf. a likely variant root with two emphatics instead of plain stops un Gez. \xe1\xb9\xada\xe1\xb8\xb3aba \xe2\x80\x98to sew together, to stitch, to mend\xe2\x80\x99 (CDG 595), Tna. \xe2\x80\x98to mend, to patch a garment\xe2\x80\x99 (TED 2447).\xc2\xa0Quite probably, the well-known PWS root *ktb ultimately belongs to the same source with metathesis. See discussion in EDA II.
