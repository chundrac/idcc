*m\xe1\xb8\xab\xc5\x9d\xcc\xa3 - to have labour pains\n (SED I No. 40v)\n

\n
\n
\n\n
\n
\n Though likely derived from PS *m\xe1\xb8\xab\xc5\x9d\xcc\xa3 \xe2\x80\x98to smite\xe2\x80\x99 (see in HALOT 571, CDG 338), a parallelism in Arb. and Eth. is hardly a fortuitous result of independent semantic development.\n
