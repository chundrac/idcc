*\xe1\xb9\xaf\xcc\xa3arb- - piece of wood\n (Kogan 2015:575)\n

\n
\n
\n\n
\n
\n

The origin is unknown. It would be tempting to identify it with Gez. \xe1\xb9\xa3araba \xe2\x80\x98to hew,\xe2\x80\x99 \xe1\xb9\xa3arb \xe2\x80\x98plank,\xe2\x80\x99 \xe1\xb9\xa3\xc9\x99rr\xc4\x81b \xe2\x80\x98splinter, shaving\xe2\x80\x99 and its cognates throughout EthS (CDG 563), but this comparison is difficult in view of reliable parallels with \xe1\xb9\xa3 elsewhere in Semitic (notably, in Soq. \xe1\xb9\xa3\xc3\xa9rob \xe2\x80\x98couper,\xe2\x80\x99 LS 357). (Kogan 2015:575)
