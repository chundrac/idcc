\n \n Akkadian\n \n
\n
\n \n i\xc5\xa1du, i\xc5\xa1tu, ildu, irdu, e\xc5\xa1du, u\xc5\xa1du - damp course, base, foundation (of a building, reign), bottom, root, lower extremities\n (CAD I 235, AHw. 393)\n \n
\n
\n
\n\n
\n May be related also to PWS *\xc5\xa1it- \xe2\x80\x98buttocks\xe2\x80\x99, see Kogan 2011: 218 Note that the word is used in particular in the dual i\xc5\xa1d\xc4\x81n which is found in\r\nAkk. usually with nouns denoting body parts or their semantic derivatives.\n
