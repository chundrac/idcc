\n \n Hebrew\n \n
\n
\n \n \xcb\x80by - to want, be willing\n (HALOT 3)\n \n
\n
\n
\n\n
\n As observed by T. N\xc3\xb6ldeke (1904:66), the Hebrew verb is almost always coupled with negative particles: l\xc5\x8d(\xcb\x80) \xcb\x80\xc4\x81b\xc4\x81 \xe2\x80\x98he did not want,\xe2\x80\x99 etc. (for a strikingly similar structural parallel in Old Assyrian l\xc4\x81 mu\xcb\x80\xc4\x81\xcb\x80um \xe2\x80\x98not to want\xe2\x80\x99 see Veenhof 1986)(Kogan 2015: 277)\n
