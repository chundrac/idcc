*d\xe1\xb8\xb3\xe1\xb8\xb3 - to crush, to pound; to be ground finely, to be fine, minute\n (EDA II)\n

\n
\n
\n\n
\n
\n

For the frequent in individual Semitic languages semantic shift \xe2\x80\x98to be ground finely, to be fine, minute\xe2\x80\x99 > \xe2\x80\x98to be little, small\xe2\x80\x99 in Indo-European languages see Buck 1949, 880ff.
