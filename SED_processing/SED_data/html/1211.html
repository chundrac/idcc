*tpp - to spit\n (SED I No. 72\xe1\xb5\xa5)\n

\n
\n
\n\n
\n
\n Rather a biconsonantal stem with various ways of triconsonantization (*tpp, *twp\r\nand *tp\xcb\x80), Cf. *twb, *tbb \xe2\x80\x98to spit, vomit\xe2\x80\x99 (SED I No. 74\xe1\xb5\xa5).\n
