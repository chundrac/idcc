*nV\xe1\xb8\xa5l- - swarm of bees\n (SED II No. 160)\n

\n
\n
\n\n
\n
\n Cf. Syr n\xc4\x81\xe1\xb8\xa5\xc9\x99lat \xe1\xb8\xb3am\xe1\xb8\xa5\xc4\x81 \xe2\x80\x98insectum quoddam\xe2\x80\x99 (LSyr. 423), \xe2\x80\x98papilio\xe2\x80\x99 (PS 2337), literally \xe2\x80\x98sifting-the-flour\xe2\x80\x99. Both elements may represent original faunal terms reinterpreted by popular etymology (cf. *\xe1\xb8\xb3Vm\xe1\xb8\xa5- \xe2\x80\x98kind of insect\xe2\x80\x99, SED II. No. 129)\n
