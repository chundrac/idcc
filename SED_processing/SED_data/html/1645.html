*pVl(y)- - kind of insect, louse\n (SED No. 175)\n

\n Clearly related is the verbal root *ply \xe2\x80\x98to delouse\xe2\x80\x99 widely attested throughout Semitic.\r\nCf. Tna. \xcb\x81af\xc3\xa4l \xe2\x80\x98insects or fleas which live in grass\xe2\x80\x99 (TED 1949). One wonders whether it could be directly compared to Akk. uplu (< *\xcb\x81upl-)?\n
