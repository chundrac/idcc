*gVnVn- - viscera; heart\n (SED I No. 83; Kogan 2015: 319)\n

\n
\n
\n\n
\n
\n Possible cognates to Ugr.-Arb. *gVnVn- \xe2\x80\x98heart, interior\xe2\x80\x99 dealt with in SED I No. 83 are rather unreliable, so it is preferable to treat this lexemr as Arb-Ugr exclusive isogloss (Kogan 2015: 319)\n
