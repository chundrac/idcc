*balV\xcb\x81/\xce\xb3-, *bal\xcb\x81Vm- - uvula; gullet, gorge\n (SED I No. 36)\n

\n
\n
\n\n
\n
\n Usually regarded as related to, or derived from, Sem. *bl\xcb\x81 \xe2\x80\x98to swallow\xe2\x80\x99 (cf. [DRS 68], [LGz. 925]); this verb, however, denitely points to \xcb\x81 (so in Arb. and MSA), while the noun under consideration shows oscillation \xcb\x81/\xce\xb3 in MSA and, likely, Arb. Note variant stems with *-m in Arb. (cf. also bl\xcb\x81m \xe2\x80\x98avaler\xe2\x80\x99 [BK 1 161]), Mnd. (unless an Arabism) and Soq., which may be regarded as common Sem., and *ta-\xc2\xad in MSA.\n
