*\xc5\x9dir- - skin, hide of cattle\n (SED I No. 267)\n

\n
\n
\n\n
\n
\n

A very complicated and problematic case. It is difficult ot say whether the Soq. ans S. Eth. parallels preserve an original biconsonantal stem, with \xcb\x81 (or \xcb\x80) added later as a triconsonantizer (*\xc5\x9dr\xcb\x81) or they have lost the original laryngeal in the position of the second or third radical. As an anatomic term attested in M. Eth. and Soq. only. Cf. *\xc5\x9dry - to skin.
