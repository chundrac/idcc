*ki/u\xe1\xb9\xaf\xcc\xa3r- - joint (of the ribs, fingers, etc.)\n (SED I No. 155)\n

\n
\n
\n\n
\n
\n Though reconstructed as an anatomic term on the PS level, the present root is likely related to, or rather derived from, Sem. *k(\xca\xb7)\xe1\xb9\xaf\xcc\xa3r \xe2\x80\x98to bind, knot, fix\xe2\x80\x99\n

\n \n ki\xe1\xb9\xa3ru - joint of the human or animal body\n (CAD k 436, AHw 488)\n \n

\n \n Biblical Aramaic\n \n
\n
\n \n *\xe1\xb8\xb3\xc9\x99\xe1\xb9\xadar - joint\n (HALOTg 1773)\n \n

\n pl. \xe1\xb8\xb3i\xe1\xb9\xadr\xc4\xabn \r\nPresumably from \xe2\x80\x98knot\xe2\x80\x99. In \xe1\xb8\xb3i\xe1\xb9\xadr\xc4\x93 \xe1\xb8\xa5ar\xe1\xb9\xa3\xc4\x93h \xe2\x80\x98the joints of his hip\xe2\x80\x99.\n
