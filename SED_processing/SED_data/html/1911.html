*labb(-at)-, *lab(y)-\xc4\x81n- - (animal's) body from neck to rump\n (SED I No. 173)\n

\n
\n
\n\n
\n
\n

Meaning somewhat diffuse (originally may denote trunk as a whole). Semantically and phonetically (\xc2\xada\xc2\xad vs. \xc2\xadi\xc2\xad) rather to be separated from *libb-\xc2\xad \xe2\x80\x98heart\xe2\x80\x99.\xc2\xa0
