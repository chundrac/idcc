*\xcb\x80Vd/d - to, towards, up to\n (Arakelova 2001:50)\n

\n
\n
\n\n
\n
\n Presumably derived from PS *yad- \xe2\x80\x98hand\xe2\x80\x99. According to Leslau, Amh. and Hrs. forms, as well as Gez. adverb wa\xcb\x80\xc9\x99da/wa\xcb\x80da/w\xc9\x99\xcb\x80da contain the deictic element w- (CDG 602).\n
