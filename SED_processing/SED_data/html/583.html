*\xcb\x81V\xc5\x9db- - grass\n (Fronzaroli 1968: 274, 289, 299; Kogan 2011: 200; Kogan 2015: 183)\n

\n
\n
\n\n
\n
\n The only possible trace of this root outside CS can be found in Akk. e\xc5\xa1\xc4\x93bu \xe2\x80\x98to grow luxuriantly\xe2\x80\x99. Its relationship to the\r\nAkkadian plant name i\xc5\xa1babtu is considerably less certain because of the peculiar morphological shape of the Akkadian word.\r\n
\r\nSab. \xcb\x81s\xe2\x82\x83bt \xe2\x80\x98pastureland\xe2\x80\x99 and Qat. \xcb\x81s\xe2\x82\x83b \xe2\x80\x98crops, produce\xe2\x80\x99,\r\noften compared to this root (Fronzaroli 1968:289), can hardly be related in view\r\nof the phonological irregularity (ESA s\xe2\x82\x83 does not normally correspond to PS \xc5\x9d)\r\n(Kagan 2015: 183)\n
