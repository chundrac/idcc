*\xcb\x80in\xc5\xa1- - man, person\n (Kogan 2015: 177, 382)\n

\n
\n
\n\n
\n
\n Loss of -n- in Hbr., Arm. and Sab. (presumably, *\xcb\x80in\xc5\xa1->*\xcb\x80\xc4\xa9\xc5\xa1->\xcb\x80\xc4\xab\xc5\xa1) remains problematic in spite of the obvious presence of -n- in the Hebrew plural form \xcb\x80\xc4\x83n\xc4\x81\xc5\xa1\xc4\xabm (constr. \xcb\x80an\xc5\xa1\xc4\x93).\n
