*\xe1\xb8\xa5bl - to be pregnant, conceive; to have labor pains\n (SED I No. 21v; Kogan 2015: 189; HALOT 286)\n

\n
\n
\n\n
\n
\n cf. SED I Nos. 110 and 21\xe1\xb5\xa5 for its possible connection with the MSA terms for umbilical cord (such as Mhr. \xe1\xb8\xa5\xc9\x99bl\xc4\x93t) and, perhaps, PS *\xe1\xb8\xa5abl- \xe2\x80\x98rope.\n
