\n \n \xc3\xa9ra\xe1\xb8\xa5 - 1. to come, to reach; 2. to be able\n (LS 74; CSOL I 476; CSOL II 392\xe2\x80\x93393; Naumkin et al. 2015a:58)\n \n

The verb \xc3\xa9ra\xe1\xb8\xa5 is fully synonymous with g\xc3\xa9da\xe1\xb8\xa5 \xe2\x80\x98to come\xe2\x80\x99, but the latter is more common. The only difference between them is the government: \xc3\xa9ra\xe1\xb8\xa5 is used with direct object, while g\xc3\xa9da\xe1\xb8\xa5 is used with the preposition di-.
