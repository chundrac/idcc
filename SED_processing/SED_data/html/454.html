*glw - to be ill\n (Kogan 2015:554)\n

\n
\n
\n\n
\n
\n The origin of Proto-MSA *glw \xe2\x80\x98to be ill\xe2\x80\x99 is unknown. Assuming that the original meaning of the root is \xe2\x80\x9cto be hot\xe2\x80\x9d, one might tentatively compare Arb. \xc7\xaflw \xe2\x80\x98to be clear, bright\xe2\x80\x99 (Kogan 2015:554)\n
