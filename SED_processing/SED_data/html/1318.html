\n \n Akkadian\n \n

\n \n par\xc4\x81\xe1\xb9\xadu - to clear away(?)\n (CAD P 180, AHw. 832, EDA I 344)\n \n
\n
\n
\n\n
\n Derived words: muparri\xe1\xb9\xadu \xe2\x80\x98food server\xe2\x80\x99 (CAD M\xe2\x82\x82 209, AHw. 1576); pir\xe1\xb9\xadu \xe2\x80\x98disposal(?)\xe2\x80\x99 (SAD I).\r\n\r\n
Insofar as the meaning \xe2\x80\x9cabrei\xc3\x9fen, -r\xc3\xa4umen\xe2\x80\x9d (AHw) suggested by von Soden is accepted, par\xc4\x81\xe1\xb9\xadu is to be derived from PS *p-r-\xe1\xb9\xad \xe2\x80\x9cto cut, to tear; to divide, to detach, to separate\xe2\x80\x9d (EDA I 344).\n
