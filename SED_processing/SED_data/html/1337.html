*bara\xe1\xb9\xa3- - scabies, tetter, ringworm\n (SED I No. 40, DRS 86, CDG 107)\n

\n
\n
\n\n
\n
\n

Common Sem. status questionable; vocalism relying on Arb. only. See discussion on a possible connection with Sem. *br\xe1\xb9\xa3 \xe2\x80\x98to shine\xe2\x80\x99 in DRS and CDG.
