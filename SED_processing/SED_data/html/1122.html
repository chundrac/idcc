*\xcb\x81imm(-a) - together with\n (Arakelova 2001:41; Huehnergard 2005:190; Hasselbach\xe2\x80\x93Huehnergard 2007:420; Kogan 2015: 182)\n

\n
\n
\n\n
\n
\n

Lipi\xc5\x84ski (1997:465) derives this preposition from the PS verbal root \xe2\x80\x98to be common\xe2\x80\x99. More cautious treatments can be found in Gesenius 1910:297 (\xe2\x80\x9d\xcb\x81im- (connexion ?) with\xe2\x80\x9d) and Tropper 2000:763 (\xe2\x80\x9d\xcb\x81m /\xcb\x81imma/ (Subst. /\xcb\x81imm-/? \xe2\x80\x9cGemeinschaft\xe2\x80\x9d im Ak.)\xe2\x80\x9d).
