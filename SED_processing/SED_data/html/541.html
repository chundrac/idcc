*\xe1\xb8\x8fVnVm-/\xe1\xb8\x8fVnVn- - rain\n (DRS 336; Kogan 2011: 193\xe2\x80\x93194)\n

\n
\n
\n\n
\n
\n Probably *\xe1\xb8\x8fnn is the original shape of the root dissimilated in EthS and Sab, but the reverse possibility (an original *\xe1\xb8\x8fnm assimilated to znn in Akkadian) cannot be excluded either (cf. DRS 336) \r\n\r\n(Kogan 2015: 442-443)\n
