*whb - to give\n (Kogan 2015:398)\n

\n
\n
\n\n
\n
\n Reflexes of PWS *whb (DRS 508) function as the main verbs with the meaning \xe2\x80\x9cto give\xe2\x80\x9d in Aramaic and most of EthS (v. CDG 609 and further remarks in Kogan 2015:70; 442).\n
