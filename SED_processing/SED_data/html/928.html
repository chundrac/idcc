*ndd - to burn (intransitive)\n (CDG 385; Kogan 2015: 463)\n

\n
\n
\n\n
\n
\n The origin of Proto-EthS *ndd \xe2\x80\x98to burn\xe2\x80\x99 is uncertain. Leslau tentatively compares Arb. nd\xcb\x80 \xe2\x80\x98to cast flesh-meat into the fire, to bury a cake of bread in hot ashes\xe2\x80\x99 (Lane 2778).\n
