*\xcb\x81alay - on, over\n (Arakelova 2001:25)\n

\n
\n
\n\n
\n
\n Throughout EthS this preposition exists only in combination with the element lV. In several languages adverbs of the *lV\xcb\x81l- type are also attested: see Syr. l\xc9\x99\xcb\x81al \xe2\x80\x98upward, above\xe2\x80\x99, Min. l-\xcb\x81l, \xe2\x80\x98upward (vers le haut)\xe2\x80\x99 (LM 31), Mnd. lilai \xe2\x80\x98upwards\xe2\x80\x99.\r\nFor the derivation from the PS root *\xcb\x81lw/*\xcb\x81ly \xe2\x80\x98to go up\xe2\x80\x99 see Lipi\xc5\x84ski 1997:466, AED 109, Tropper 2000:766.\n

\n \n Hebrew\n \n
\n
\n \n \xcb\x81al - on, over\n (HALOT 825)\n \n
\n
\n
\n\n
\n Also in front of, before; more than; on the side of, supported by; on account of; with regard to, concerning; according to; against, opposite; to, towards; in addition to, together with; from far off. The form \xcb\x81\xc7\x8el\xc4\x93 (< *\xcb\x81alay) attested in poetic texts corresponds directly to the protoform.\n
