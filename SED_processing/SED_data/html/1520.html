*\xe1\xb9\xa3aprir- - flower\n (Kogan 2015:570)\n

\n
\n
\n\n
\n
\n The origin of Proto-MSA *\xe1\xb9\xa3aprir- \xe2\x80\x98flower\xe2\x80\x99 is uncertain. Leslau\xe2\x80\x99s comparison with Syr. \xe1\xb9\xa3apr\xc4\x81 \xe2\x80\x98tempus matutinum\xe2\x80\x99 (LSyr. 635), implying an original meaning \xe2\x80\x9cs\xe2\x80\x99\xc3\xa9chapper, \xc3\xa9clater\xe2\x80\x9d is not easily acceptable from the semantic point of view. Hardly any connection with Arb. \xcb\x80a\xe1\xb9\xa3far- \xe2\x80\x98yellow\xe2\x80\x99 (Lane 1699). Of some interest is Akk. \xe1\xb9\xa3ipru \xe2\x80\x98crest, coma (of a comet), summit, excrescence\xe2\x80\x99 (CAD \xe1\xb9\xa2 204, AHw. 1104). (Kogan 2015:570)\n
