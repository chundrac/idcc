*b\xc5\xa1l - to be cooked, ready, ripe\n (Fronzaroli 1971: 626, 636, 642; Kogan 2011: 242)\n

\n
\n
\n\n
\n
\n The transitive meaning \xe2\x80\x98to boil, cook\xe2\x80\x99 is mostly reserved for causative formations: Akk. \xc5\xa1ub\xc5\xa1ulu, Hbr. bi\xc5\xa1\xc5\xa1\xc4\x93l, Syr. ba\xc5\xa1\xc5\xa1el, Arb. \xcb\x80absala \xe2\x80\x98to boil unripe dates\xe2\x80\x99 (TA 28 84), Gez. \xcb\x80absala, Mhr. h\xc9\x99bh\xc5\x8dl, Jib. eb\xc5\xa1\xc3\xa9l, Soq. \xcb\x80\xc3\xa9bhel, probably Ugr. b\xc5\xa1l (DUL 242).\n
