*gapp-, *\xcb\x80a-gapp- - wing\n (SED I No. 88)\n

\n
\n
\n\n
\n

\n \n Akkadian\n \n

\n \n gappu - wing\n (CAD K 185, AHw. 281)\n \n
\n
\n
\n\n
\n Var. agappu.
\r\nIdentified by CAD with kappu, but treated as a separate entry in AHw, which seems right in view of the comparative data; note that in AHw agappu is also treated as a separate gloss independent of gappu (AHw 15).\n
