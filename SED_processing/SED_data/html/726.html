\n \n Proto-MSA\n \n
\n
\n \n *\xe1\xb9\xaf\xcc\xa3a(h)r - on, over\n (Kogan 2015:575)\n \n
\n
\n
\n\n
\n

The original anatomical meaning is completely lost throughout this group. Semantically similar prepositional use of reflexes of *\xe1\xb9\xaf\xcc\xa3a(h)r- is admittedly attested in a few other Semitic languages (SED I No. 284). (Kogan 2015:575)
