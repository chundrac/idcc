\n \n PWS\n \n
\n
\n \n *\xe1\xb8\xabsr - to be deficient; to lose\n (DRS 1012; Kogan 2015: 86)\n \n
\n
\n
\n\n
\n

PWS *\xe1\xb8\xabsr \xe2\x80\x98to be deficient; to lose\xe2\x80\x99 has been often compared to Akk. \xe1\xb8\xabes\xc4\x93ru \xe2\x80\x98to blunt, to chip, to trim\xe2\x80\x99. If accepted, this comparison certainly implies the priority of the Akkadian meaning and, hence, points to a shared semanitc innovation in PWS
