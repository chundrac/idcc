\n An original Akk. lexeme which may be related to PS *\xcb\x81Vn\xe1\xb8\xb3- with a meaning shift from \xe2\x80\x98necklace\xe2\x80\x99, see SED I No. 15, Kogan 2011:216. Note, however, that unqu means \xe2\x80\x98fingerring\xe2\x80\x99.\n
