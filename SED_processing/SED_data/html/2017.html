*d\xe1\xb8\xb3d\xe1\xb8\xb3 - to pound; to be fine, small\n

\n
\n
\n\n
\n
\n

A variant root of *d\xe1\xb8\xb3\xe1\xb8\xb3 attested in WS in both transitive and intransitive (adjectival) verbs as well as in derived nouns. \xc2\xa0
