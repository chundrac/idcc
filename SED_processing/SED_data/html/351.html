\n \n Akkadian\n \n
\n
\n \n \xe1\xb8\xabu\xe1\xb9\xad\xc4\x81ru - stick, staff\n (CAD \xe1\xb8\xaa 264, AHw. 362)\n \n
\n
\n
\n\n
\n Var. \xe1\xb8\xabu\xe1\xb9\xadartu. Attested in NB/NA and Nuzi, which almost certainly suggests a foreign origin. An Aramaism is quite feasible in NB and NA but, of course, not so likely in Nuzi. If this is nevertheless the case, it would be one of the earliest West Semitisms with marked Aramaic traits in Akkadian.\n
