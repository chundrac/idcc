*\xcb\x81\xc4\x81d- - (he is) still\n (Kogan 2015: 76\xe2\x80\x9377, 105)\n

\n
\n
\n\n
\n
\n likely candidate for a PWS lexical innovation, ultimately connected with the verbal root *\xcb\x81wd \xe2\x80\x98to turn\xe2\x80\x99 It is hard to avoid thinking, however, that the functional equivalent of *\xcb\x81\xc4\x81d- in Akkadian, namely ad\xc4\xabni \xe2\x80\x98until now; not yet\xe2\x80\x99, has something to do with it also in terms of etymology (Kogan 2015: 76)\n
