*\xc5\xa1um\xc5\xa1um- - ant\n (SED II No. 205)\n

\n
\n
\n\n
\n
\n Cf. Tgr. \xc5\xa1a\xc5\xa1\xc9\x99nte \xe2\x80\x98ants\xe2\x80\x99 (WTS 214), probably related with the first m lost and the second one assimilated to n before t (for an alternative interpretation of this form v. *s\xc4\x81/\xc5\xabs- \xe2\x80\x98moth\xe2\x80\x99, SED II No. 198).\r\nCf. Sod. ma\xc5\xa1o \xe2\x80\x98kind of ant\xe2\x80\x99 (EDG 433). Metathesis of a non-reduplicated prototype of the present root?\n
