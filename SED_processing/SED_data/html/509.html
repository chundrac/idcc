\n \n Akkadian\n \n
\n
\n \n d\xc4\x81du - paternal uncle\n (Kogan 2014:91\xe2\x80\x9392)\n \n
\n
\n
\n\n
\n The term belongs to the \xe2\x80\x9cAmorite\xe2\x80\x9d system of kin designations and is attested mainly in Mari corpus; Akkadian proper employs analytical expressions to designate uncle and aunt, e.g. a\xe1\xb8\xabi abim, a\xe1\xb8\xab\xc4\x81t abim and so on.\n
