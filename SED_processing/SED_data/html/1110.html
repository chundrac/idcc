*np\xe1\xb8\xab - to blow, breathe, inflate\n (SED I No. 45v)\n

\n
\n
\n\n
\n
\n Eventually derived from *p\xe1\xb8\xab/\xe1\xb8\xa5 \xe2\x80\x98to breath, blow, exhale (smell)\xe2\x80\x99 (SED I No. 54v) by prefixation of *n\xc2\xad.\n
