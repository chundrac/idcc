*li\xc5\xa1(\xc5\xa1)\xc4\x81n- - tongue\n (SED I No. 181)\n

\n
\n
\n\n
\n
\n

\n \n lasoun-, lasim- - tongue\n (DNWSI 584)\n \n

\n
\n\n
\n The 1st form occurs in Dioscurides as a part of a plant name \xce\xbb\xce\xb1\xcf\x83\xce\xbf\xcf\x85\xce\xbd\xce\xb1\xcf\x86 as an equivalent of Gr. \xce\xb2\xce\xbf\xcf\x8d\xce\xb3\xce\xbb\xcf\x89\xcf\x83\xcf\x83\xce\xbf\xce\xbd. The 2nd one (also a plant name) is attested in Pseudo Apulei Platonici Herbarius as lasimsaph.\n
