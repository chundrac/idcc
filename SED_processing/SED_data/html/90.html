\n \n \xc3\xa9da\xcb\x81 - to know\n (JL 286)\n \n
\n
\n
\n\n
\n \xce\xb3\xc3\xa1r\xc9\x94\xcc\x81b is the neutral verb for the meaning \xe2\x80\x9cto know\xe2\x80\x9d, while \xc3\xa9da\xcb\x81 is attested in SAE VII mostly in negative sentences with the preposition b-. In the JLO coprus the use of \xc3\xa9da\xcb\x81 is restricted to negative sentences, propositional phrases and the phrase \xc3\xa9da\xcb\x81 b- \xe2\x80\x98to be aware of\xe2\x80\x99 (see Kogan 2015:507 for examples).\n
