*\xe1\xb8\xa5am\xe1\xb9\xaf- - lower belly, uterus, womb\n (SED I No. 122; Kogan 2011: 217)\n

\n
\n
\n\n
\n
\n Phonetically not without difficulties: only Ugr. and MSA forms definitely point\r\nto *-\xe1\xb9\xaf as the third radical while reflexes in other languages are either not indicative\r\n(Akk., Hbr., M. Eth.) or irregular (Arm.).\n
