*\xe1\xb9\xadad - one\n (Kogan 2015:543)\n

\n
\n
\n\n
\n
\n \xe2\x80\x9cIdentical to Qat. \xe1\xb9\xadd \xe2\x80\x98one\xe2\x80\x99 (LIQ 77), but one may doubt that this completely isolated term is autochthonous in ESA (rather, an MSA substratum or adstratum influence has to be surmised). There is hardly any trace of PS *\xcb\x80a\xe1\xb8\xa5ad- \xe2\x80\x98one\xe2\x80\x99 in MSA. Further background of *\xe1\xb9\xadad- is enigmatic. One may be tempted to follow M. Bittner who analyzes it as an amalgamation of *\xcb\x80a\xe1\xb8\xa5ad- with some other element (but hardly the relative pronoun *\xe1\xb8\x8fV- as Bittner assumes.\xe2\x80\x9d (Kogan 2015:543)\n
