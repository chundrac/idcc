PArm
\n
\n

*m\xc4\x81ri\xcb\x80- - lord\n (Kogan 2015:396)\n

\n
\n
\n\n
\n
\n PArm. *m\xc4\x81ri\xcb\x80- \xe2\x80\x98lord\xe2\x80\x99 goes back to PS *mar\xcb\x80- \xe2\x80\x98man, male\xe2\x80\x99 (Fronzaroli 1964:28\xe2\x80\x9229, 42). This term has gradually replaced PS *ba\xcb\x81l- (HALOT 142, DUL 206), which is mostly relegated to the meaning \xe2\x80\x9chusband\xe2\x80\x9d in later Aramaic dialects (DJPA 108, LSyr. 83, MD 60).\n
