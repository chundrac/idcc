*\xcb\x80Vn\xc4\x81\xc5\xa1- - man\n (Kogan 2015:392)\n

\n
\n
\n\n
\n
\n Application of PCS (or PWS) *\xcb\x80Vn\xc4\x81\xc5\xa1- to one single individual (\xe2\x80\x9cperson,\xe2\x80\x9d \xe2\x80\x9c(some)one\xe2\x80\x9d) \xe2\x80\x93 to some extent combined with the generic sense \xe2\x80\x9cmankind\xe2\x80\x9d and the collective meaning \xe2\x80\x9cpeople\xe2\x80\x9d \xe2\x80\x93 is a highly peculiar Aramaic feature with no obvious precedents elsewhere in Semitic. However, see Tgr. \xcb\x80\xc9\x99nas \xe2\x80\x98man\xe2\x80\x99, Sab. Min. Qat. \xcb\x80ns\xe2\x82\x81 (SD 6, LM 6, LIQ 13).\n
