*p\xc5\xa1r - to tear loose, to melt\n (EDA I 452)\n

\n
\n
\n\n
\n
\n The meanings \xe2\x80\x9cto interpret, to explain\xe2\x80\x9d, broadly attested for this root throughout WS, must be due to Akkadian influence (Wagner 1966, 96), either immediately or with various intermediaries (Akkadian > Aramaic > Arabic > MSA)\n
