*pakk- - jaw, joint of the jaw\n (SED I No. 212)\n

\n
\n
\n\n
\n
\n Rather scarce but reliable attestation. -n- in one of the Arb. forms may imply Sem. *pank- > *pakk-. Cf. a variant root *pagg- \xe2\x80\x98jaw\xe2\x80\x99: Hbr. pB. pag (Jastrow 1132); Jud. pagga (ibid.), Syr. pagga (LSyr. 555).\n
