*\xcb\x81bd - to serve (?), to work, to make\n (Huehnergard 1995: 276; Kogan 2015: 380)\n

\n
\n
\n\n
\n
\n The meaning \xe2\x80\x9cto serve\xe2\x80\x9d is probably the original one (cf. Huehnergard 1995:276); \r\n
\r\nA denominative verb from *\xcb\x81abd- rather than vice versa (Kogan 2015: 181)\n
