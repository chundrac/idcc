*t\xc9\x99nan - smoke\n (Kogan 2015:397)\n

\n
\n
\n\n
\n
\n The OArm. spelling with t (rather than \xc5\xa1) makes unlikely the relationship between PArm. *t\xc9\x99nan and Hbr. \xcb\x81\xc4\x81\xc5\xa1\xc4\x81n, Arb. \xcb\x81a\xe1\xb9\xafan- \xe2\x80\x98smoke\xe2\x80\x99 (HALOT 896, Lane 1954) assumed in LSyr. 828 and Beyer 1984:419 and corroborates its connection with Gez. tann \xe2\x80\x98smoke\xe2\x80\x99 and its cognates in modern EthS (CDG 577).\n
