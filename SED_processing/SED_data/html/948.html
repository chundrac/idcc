*hll - to be imbecile, weak-minded, mad; be mocked at\n (SED I No. 19\xe1\xb5\xa5)\n

\n
\n
\n\n
\n
\n Originally rather a biconsonantal stem. Note various ways of triconsonantization in Eth. and a full reduplication in Arb. and Soq.\n
