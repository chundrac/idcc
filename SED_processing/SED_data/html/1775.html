*\xe1\xb8\xabar\xe1\xb9\xad\xc5\xabm- - (long) nose, beak, trunk, etc.\n (SED I No. 137)\n

\n
\n
\n\n
\n
\n Likely derived from *\xe1\xb8\xabu\xe1\xb9\xadm-\xc2\xad \xe2\x80\x98nose, snout, muzzle, beak\xe2\x80\x99 (No. 139) through insertion of \xc2\xadr\xc2\xad; one wonders whether it may be the result of metathesis of \xc2\xadr suffixed or of contamination with some unidentified root. C. Sem. only unless Akk. \xe1\xb8\xabu\xe1\xb9\xad\xe1\xb9\xadimmu \xe2\x80\x98snout, muzzle\xe2\x80\x99 SB on [CAD \xe1\xb8\xaa 265] is an assimilated form from *\xe1\xb8\xabur\xe1\xb9\xadimmu (otherwise see in *\xe1\xb8\xabu\xe1\xb9\xadm- \xe2\x80\x98nose, snout, muzzle, beak\xe2\x80\x99, No. 139).\n
