\n
\n
\n\n
\n
\n

Cf. a related verb *nbl \xe2\x80\x98to decay, die\xe2\x80\x99. Cf. Sem.\xc2\xa0*npl\xc2\xa0- to fall\xc2\xa0(e. g. in [HALOT 709]) and such derived forms, probably developed by contamination with the present root, as Hbr. mapp\xc3\xa4l\xc3\xa4t \xe2\x80\x98carcass\xe2\x80\x99 [HALOT 618] (< *ma-npal\xc2\xad-t-\xc2\xad), Syr. mappult\xc4\x81\xc2\xa0\xe2\x80\x98cadaver\xe2\x80\x99 [LSyr. 847] (< *ma-npul-t\xc2\xad-).
