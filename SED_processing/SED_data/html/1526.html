*\xc5\xa1Vkay- - sword\n (Kogan 2015:571)\n

\n
\n
\n\n
\n
\n

The origin is unknown. Contra M. Bittner (1915b:28) and W. Leslau, hardly any connection with Arb. \xc5\xa1kk \xe2\x80\x98to pierce with the spear\xe2\x80\x99, since Arb. \xc5\xa1 does not regularly correspond to \xc5\xa1 in MSA. Note Tgr. saky\xc3\xa4t \xe2\x80\x98iron-chips that fall off when a sword is whetted; edge of a sword\xe2\x80\x99. (Kogan 2015:571)
