\n \n Geez\n \n
\n
\n \n \xe1\xb8\xabarasa - to bear a child, lie in bed when giving birth to a child\n (CDG 264)\n \n
\n
\n
\n\n
\n The forms \xe1\xb8\xabara\xc5\x9da, \xe1\xb8\xa5ara\xc5\x9da are graphic variants.\n
\n
\n\n
\n
\n
\n
\n \n Geez\n \n
\n
\n \n \xe1\xb8\xabara\xc5\x9da, \xe1\xb8\xa5arasa, \xe1\xb8\xabarrasa - to feed, nourish, take good care of someone by properly feeding him\n (CDG 264)\n \n
\n
\n
\n\n
\n See Leslau\xe2\x80\x99s comment: \xe2\x80\x9c\xe1\xb8\xabarasa is separated here from \xe1\xb8\xabara\xc5\x9da\xe2\x80\xa6 but in fact it is probably the same root with a different spelling and means \xe2\x80\x98to bear a child\xe2\x80\x99 and \xe2\x80\x98to feed (or take care of) the woman in childbed.\xe2\x80\x9d\n
