*\xe1\xb8\xb3ny - to acquire, to possess\n (Kogan 2015: 89)\n

\n
\n
\n\n
\n
\n While both standard Akkadian dictionaries display a verb \xe1\xb8\xb3an\xc3\xbb \xe2\x80\x98to acquire\xe2\x80\x99\r\n(CAD Q 91, AHw. 898), there are weighty reasons to doubt that an autochthonous\r\nAkkadian lexeme with this meaning has ever existed. As for the numerous NA attestations\r\n(including the noun \xe1\xb8\xb3in\xc4\xabtu, Deller 1991), they are most probably to be\r\nexplained by Aramaic influence\n
