\n \n Akkadian\n \n
\n
\n \n summu - dove\n (CAD S 380, AHw. 1058)\n \n
\n
\n
\n\n
\n From OB on. Fem. summatu (Var. simmatu, \xc5\xa1ummatu, \xc5\xa1immatu) \xe2\x80\x94 from OAkk. on. The fem. form is by far more widely attested from OB on (in OAkk. as a personal name only). The masc. form is less common, attested mostly in literary texts (e.g. UET 6 396.25 idammumam k\xc4\xabma su-mi-[i]m \xe2\x80\x98he moans like a dove\xe2\x80\x99, OB). Both forms are discussed in Salonen 1973 254-8.\n
