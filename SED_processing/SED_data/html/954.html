*\xe1\xb8\xa5lm - to dream; have nocturnal ejaculations when dreaming\n (SED I No. 25\xe1\xb5\xa5; HALOT 320)\n

\n
\n
\n\n
\n
\n There is no trace of *\xe1\xb8\xa5lm \xe2\x80\x98to dream\xe2\x80\x99 in Akkadian, where the nominal\r\nconcept \xe2\x80\x9cdream\xe2\x80\x9d is expressed by \xc5\xa1uttu, a secondary\r\nformation from PS *\xc5\xa1in-at- \xe2\x80\x98sleep (Kogan 2015: 83)\n
