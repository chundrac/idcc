*b\xc4\x81b- - door, gate\n (EDA I 58)\n

\n
\n
\n\n
\n
\n

Borrowed from Akkadian (often via Aramaic and Arabic) into a wide range of Semitic languages. The origin of the Akkadian lexeme is uncertain.\xc2\xa0
