\n \n f\xc9\x99\xe1\xb8\xb3r\xc3\xa9ro - neck\n (LS 340, CSOL I 534)\n \n

\n

In Soqotri f\xc9\x99\xe1\xb8\xb3r\xc3\xa9ro is the main designation of \xe2\x80\x9cneck\xe2\x80\x9d, it is applied to the external neck only. The word \xe1\xb8\xb3ar (LS 384) can be applied to both \xe2\x80\x9cneck\xe2\x80\x9d (external) and \xe2\x80\x9cthroat\xe2\x80\x9d.
