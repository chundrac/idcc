*na\xe1\xb8\xab\xc4\xabr- - nostril\n (SED I No. 198; Kogan 2011: 221)\n

\n
\n
\n\n
\n
\n

The reconstructed vocalic pattern is based on Akk., Hbr., Arm. and rather supported by MSA *na\xe1\xb8\xabr\xc4\xabr-; cf., however, -\xc2\xadu-\xc2\xad in Arb. nu\xe1\xb8\xab(a)rat\xc2\xad- and Jud. n\xe1\xb8\xabwr.\xc2\xa0
