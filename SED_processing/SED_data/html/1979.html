\n \n Akkadian\n \n
\n
\n \n \xc5\xa1ullu - a pockmark or wart\n (CAD \xc5\xa1 3, 241; AHw. 1266 )\n \n
\n
\n
\n\n
\n

Exact reconstruction of the sibilant is difficult since Akk.\xc2\xa0\xc5\xa1\xc2\xa0does not correspond to Hbr. and Jud. s (note that the irregularities of PS sibilant reflexation in Akk. observed elsewhere are confined to the reverse situation: Akk. s instead of the expected \xc5\xa1).Cf.\xc2\xa0\xc5\xa1\xc5\xablu \xe2\x80\x98a partial or complete perforation\xe2\x80\x99 [CAD \xc5\xa1 2 451], [AHw 1237]. This word, found mostly in descriptions of internal organs, is once used to denote a feature of a newborn\xe2\x80\x99s appearance and can be plausibly understood as a kind of skin eruption identical to \xc5\xa1ullu (\xc5\xa1\xc5\xablu), cf. discussion in [Holma 1912 22].\xc2\xa0
