*wa\xe1\xb9\xa3il- - joint, articulation (of the humerus)\n (SED I No. 289)\n

\n
\n
\n\n
\n
\n As a nominal root with anatomic meaning, C. Sem. only.\r\nTraditionally regarded (for example, in HALOT 82) as deverbal of Sem. *\xcb\x80/w\xe1\xb9\xa3l ; the connection is quite plausible. For this verbal root see DRS 593.\n
