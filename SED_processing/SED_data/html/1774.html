\n \n Tigre\n \n
\n
\n \n \xe1\xb8\xa5\xc9\x99f\xc9\x99n - hoof\n (WTS 104)\n \n

\n\n
\n Questionable. Though fully coinciding with \xe1\xb8\xa5\xc9\x99f\xc9\x99n \xe2\x80\x98both hands full\xe2\x80\x99 (see *\xe1\xb8\xa5a/upn-\xc2\xad \xe2\x80\x98hollow of the hand, handful\xe2\x80\x99, No. 125), has probably a different origin because of the semantic difference; one wonders whether the underlying form can be *\xe1\xb8\xa5ufn, with a secondary \xc2\xad-n (an old suffix or a result of contamination with \xe1\xb8\xa5\xc9\x99f\xc9\x99n \xe2\x80\x98both hands full\xe2\x80\x99?).\n
