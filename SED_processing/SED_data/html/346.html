PArm
\n
\n

*\xe1\xb9\xafawr-(at)- - cow, bull\n (SED II No. 241, Kogan 2015:400)\n

\n
\n
\n\n
\n
\n

No feminine formation from PS *\xe1\xb9\xafawr- \xe2\x80\x98bull, ox\xe2\x80\x99 is attested outside PArm. *\xe1\xb9\xafawr-at-. The only exception is Arb. \xe1\xb9\xafawrat-, but this rare word is certainly no basic designation of \xe2\x80\x9ccow\xe2\x80\x9d in Arabic (in TA 10 338, only one verse by al-A\xe1\xb8\xab\xe1\xb9\xadal is adduced as an illustration, which may testify to the restricted use of this lexeme).
