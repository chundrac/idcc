\n \n Proto-MSA\n \n
\n
\n \n *minsab- - pubic hair\n (Kogan 2015:565)\n \n
\n
\n
\n\n
\n The origin of Proto-MSA *minsab- \xe2\x80\x98pubic hair\xe2\x80\x99 is uncertain, for a few tentative comparisons v. SED I No. 239. The similarity between the MSA terms and Sum. munsub \xe2\x80\x98hair\xe2\x80\x99 (Civil 2007:29) is striking, but hard to explain. (Kogan 2015:565)\n
