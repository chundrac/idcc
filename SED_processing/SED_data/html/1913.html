*lu\xce\xb3(\xce\xb3)- - throat\n (SED I No. 176 )\n

\n
\n
\n\n
\n
\n

Though attested scarcely and with meaning shifts, reliable enough to be separated from homonymous\xc2\xa0*lV\xce\xb3-\xc2\xa0\xe2\x80\x98jaw\xe2\x80\x99 for semantic reasons.

\r\n\r\n

There is a series of derived verbal forms with meanings connected with throat, most of which are likely to have developed independently in different languages.\xc2\xa0
