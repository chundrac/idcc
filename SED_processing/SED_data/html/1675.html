*ra\xcb\x80/w/hy- - kind of bird\n (SED II No. 187)\n

\n
\n
\n\n
\n
\n Phonetically and semantically rather diverse.\r\nAkk. ru \xe2\x80\x98eagle\xe2\x80\x99 attested in Hh. XIV 137 (= A.MUSEN) is hardly relevant for comparison: as rightly suggested in CAD R 392, it must be \xe2\x80\x9c variant, perhaps erroneous, for aru\xe2\x80\x9d (present in another variant of the same entry of Hh.).\n
