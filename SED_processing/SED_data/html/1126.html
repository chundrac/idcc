PS
\n
\n

*bVl(Vy) - without\n (Kogan 2015: 184)\n

\n
\n
\n\n
\n
\n The PCS negative particle *bal is likely related to this preposition (Arakelova 2001:25, Kogan 2015:184). There is also a possibility that *bVl(Vy) \xe2\x80\x98without\xe2\x80\x99 is ultimately connected to the PS root *bly \xe2\x80\x98to be worn out\xe2\x80\x99 (GAG \xc2\xa7 114 r, Tropper 2000:771, Kogan 2015:184), although this is not generally accepted (Arakelova 2001:25, Pat-El 2013).\n
