*\xcb\x80il/\xcb\x80ilay - towards\n (Arakelova 2001:36, Huehnergard 2005:190, Hasselbach\xe2\x80\x93Huehnergard 2007:420; Kogan 2015: 180)\n

\n
\n
\n\n
\n
\n E. Lipi\xc5\x84ski (1997:461) assumes an eventual connection with PWS *li\r\n\xe2\x80\x98to, towards\xe2\x80\x99\r\n\r\nPossibly related are MSA forms (Bla\xc5\xbeek 2007:24\xe2\x80\x9225): Hrs. w\xc9\x99l \xe2\x80\x98towards\xe2\x80\x99, Jib. y\xc9\x94l \xe2\x80\x98towards\xe2\x80\x99, Soq. a\xe1\xb8\xb7 (more often in di-\xcb\x80\xc3\xa1\xe1\xb8\xb7) \xe2\x80\x98towards\xe2\x80\x99. The etymological background of the MSA forms is, however, rather uncertain, cf. Mhr. h\xc4\x81l \xe2\x80\x98to, with, into\xe2\x80\x99 and t\xc9\x99w\xc5\x8dli \xe2\x80\x98to, towards\xe2\x80\x99.\r\n\r\nHar. il\xc4\x81, il\xc4\x81wa are likely borrowed from Arabic, although -wa in il\xc4\x81wa is difficult to explain (Arakelova 2001:37, Kogan 2015:181).\n
