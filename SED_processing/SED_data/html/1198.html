*n\xc5\xa1m - to breathe\n (SED I No. 50\xe1\xb5\xa5; Kogan 2015: 217)\n

\n
\n
\n\n
\n
\n The origin is uncertain, but, as surmised already in LSyr. 451 and HALOT 730, this root cannot be separated from *n\xc5\xa1b and *n\xc5\xa1p, perhaps also *np\xc5\xa1 with comparable meanings (cf. SED I Nos. 47\xe1\xb5\xa5,\r\n49\xe1\xb5\xa5, 51\xe1\xb5\xa5).\n
