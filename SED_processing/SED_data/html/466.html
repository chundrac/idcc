\n
Proto-MSA
\n
\n

*hnd - to be sleepy\n (Kogan 2015:557)\n

\n
\n
\n\n
\n
\n The origin of Proto-MSA \xe2\x80\x98to be sleepy\xe2\x80\x99 is uncertain. The authors of DRS 431 are probably correct to identify it with Arb. hnd (II) \xe2\x80\x98to be slow in doing, to be reluctant to do something\xe2\x80\x99, Tgr. h\xc9\x99n\xc9\x99d g\xc3\xa4\xcb\x80a \xe2\x80\x98to be lazy, amazed\xe2\x80\x99 (Kogan 2015:557)\n
