\n \n Akkadian\n \n
\n
\n \n dab\xc3\xbb - bear\n (CAD D 17, AHw. 148)\n \n
\n
\n
\n\n
\n Attested in a few SB literary texts and lexical lists, in earlier periods in proper names only. Cf. further Landsberger 1934: 82-3, Salonen 1976: 194-5. Geminated -b- i n da-ab-bu (Malku V 50) is probably due to a WS influence.\n
